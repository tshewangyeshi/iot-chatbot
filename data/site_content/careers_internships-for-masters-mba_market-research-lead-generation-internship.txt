Lead Acquisition & Market Insights Intern 



Overview:As a Lead Acquisition & Market Insights Intern at IoT Manufacturing Tech, you will gain valuable experience in identifying market opportunities, conducting industry research, and generating qualified leads. This internship is ideal for MBA and Master’s students who want to explore business development, market analysis, and sales strategies in the management industry. 



Location: RemoteJob Type: Internship 



Key Responsibilities:Study the market to find new industries and clients (e.g., logistics, healthcare).Look at trends, technology, and competitors to identify growth.Reach out to leads using emails, calls, and social media.Keep lead information updated and work with the sales team for follow-up.Share reports and insights on market activity. 



Skills & Qualifications:Current MBA or Master’s student in Business Administration, Marketing, or related field.Good research, communication, and analysis skills.Knowledge of Excel, Word, and PowerPoint.Able to manage multiple tasks.Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines.
