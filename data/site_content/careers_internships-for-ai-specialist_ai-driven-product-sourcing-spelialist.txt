AI-Driven Product Sourcing Spelialist 



Job Location: Virtual/Remote  
Duration: 3 to 6 months 
Responsibilities

Identify trends, potential suppliers, and new product opportunities using market intelligence tools, dashboards, and competitor analysis platforms.
Collaborate with internal teams such as product developers, data engineers, and procurement automation specialists.
Maintain digital records in compatible formats; generate reports using analytics tools (e.g., Power BI, Tableau) to track metrics like supplier performance and delivery timelines.
Participate in sourcing projects, including hardware components for tech product pipelines.
Explore smart logistics, autonomous restocking, and sourcing automation within the supply chain.
Utilize tools for supplier analysis, product comparison, and demand forecasting.
Support workflow automation through chatbots, contract parsing tools, and RPA bots for repetitive tasks.

Qualifications

Currently pursuing a degree in Technology, Business, Supply Chain Management, Data Analytics, or a related field.
Strong communication skills and the ability to collaborate across automated supply chain systems.
Eagerness to learn and contribute to a digitally transformed sourcing process.
Proficient in Microsoft Office; knowledge of Excel add-ins, ERP systems (e.g., SAP, Oracle), and automation tools is a plus.

Benefits

Gain hands-on experience in sourcing with exposure to smart supply chain technologies.
Contribute to projects involving digital twins, predictive procurement systems, and intelligent warehouse tools.
Receive 3 certificates upon completing the internship.Flexible, remote work structure enhancing employability in the tech-driven job market.
